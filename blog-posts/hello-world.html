Hello, World!

  • General

April 25th, 2026

I was browsing my boss's personal site this week, and it was so inspiring that I remembered I had one, too. When I checked it, I discovered it hadn't been updated since last year! Even worse, the portfolio section hadn't been touched since I was hired at my current job.

That realization brought me to this point. I decided it was time to update my information and my portfolio, but then I realized I hadn't produced much personal work over the past couple of years.

It's time for a personal project, right? I figured I should start writing again---which only makes sense, considering "Writer" is in my job title. I wanted to find something that wasn't overly time-consuming but would allow me to create using my existing skills (now powered by AI, which wasn't nearly as prominent when I first started) while getting back into the habit of writing.

Hence, this blog page! Let's see where this goes.
